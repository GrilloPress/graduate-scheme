We're launching an exciting new graduate management scheme to grow and develop passionate, inspirational leaders of the future.

Over two years, you'll gain the skills, knowledge and confidence that will enable you to make real improvements at the frontline of the healthcare service and improve patient care.

About us

Sheffield Teaching Hospitals is one of the UK’s most successful NHS foundation trusts. We provide a full range of local hospital and community services for people in Sheffield, as well as specialist care for patients from further afield, including cancer, spinal cord injuries, renal and cardiothoracic services.

We have an ambitious vision to be recognised as the best provider of health, clinical research and education in the UK and a strong contributor to the aspiration of Sheffield to be a vibrant and healthy city.

Join us as a graduate management trainee and help make that vision a reality!

The scheme

We’re offering a two year management training programme which will provide you with a rich experience helping lead the future of the NHS at Sheffield Teaching Hospitals.

The scheme will provide you with the skills, experiences and personal qualities you need to be a big success. Supported every step of the way by a buddy, mentor and our managers, you’ll take on new responsibilities from the very start and be given the chance to manage teams and projects.

During the scheme you’ll also be enrolled in the NHS Leadership Academy Mary Seacole programme and provided with their formal six month leadership development programme.

About you

Doing the right thing for our patients, staff and the wider public and is what drives us and we’ll expect you to share our values. Successful candidates will:

  • Be excellent communicators and able to form productive relationships and networks across departments within the Trust
  • Have excellent organisational skills
  • Be a self-starter and able to work on own initiative
  • Have a real interest in the NHS and a desire to contribute to its future success
  • Have a desire to learn new skills and undertake formal study leading to management and leadership qualifications

We are looking for strong team players from a variety of backgrounds, committed to the values of the NHS, with the passion, drive and ability to influence and deliver at all levels.

We are committed to recruiting people from a range of different backgrounds and would particularly welcome applications from candidate’s underrepresented in leadership roles.

The 2 year programme will commence on 16 October 2017 and all successful candidates must be available to commence in post on this date.

Application process

1.

Online application

Your application must be submitted via the NHS Jobs by completing the online application form. Candidates are encouraged to read the associated documentation prior to completing and submitting their application.

Applications will be reviewed within 14 days from the advertisement being closed. If you successfully demonstrate how you meet the essential criteria you will be shortlisted to progress further through the application process.

2.

Assessment centre

Shortlisted candidates will progress through the selection process, including attendance at an assessment centre on the 14 July 2017.

We will select 30 candidates to progress through to the assessment centre stage. During the assessment centre, participants will be required to complete a variety of exercises and will undergo a panel interview.

3.

Pre-employment checks

Within 7 days of the assessment centre, the successful trainees will be informed that they have secured a conditional offer of a place on the graduate trainee management scheme.

A programme of clearance checks will commence for the successful candidates and upon successful clearance through pre-employment checks your confirmed offer of employment will be made.
